# Questions

a. Which gender group is extremely more likely to adopt the autonomous vehicle?¶

From the gender cross tabulation, the males have a higher likelihood of adopting the autonomous vehicles. There are more males responding positively to the adoption of the technology as compared to more females disregarding the idea of adoption of autonomous driving.

b. What is the relationship between adopting autonomous vehicles and income?¶

From the income crosstabulation, generally most people are unlikely to adopt autonomous vehicles regardless of their income levels. The scatter chart figure 2 shows that a large percentage of people earning over $40k are not sure of the adoption of autonomous vehicles.

c. What is the relationship between adopting autonomous vehicles and age?¶

The youth and mid age group are likely to adopt autonomous vehicles. Old people are unlikey to adopt the technology.
